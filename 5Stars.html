BUTTER LOBSTER
  • Ingredients:
    • 4 (1 1/4-pound) live lobsters
    • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
    • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    • 1/4 cup dry white wine
    • 1/4 cup lemon juice
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Instructions:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the lobsters and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until they turn bright red.
    • Remove the lobsters from the pot and let them cool slightly.
    • Crack the lobster shells and remove the meat.
    • In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, or until fragrant.
    • Add the parsley, wine, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
    • Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until slightly thickened.
    • Add the lobster meat to the sauce and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until heated through.
    • Serve immediately with crusty bread for dipping.
